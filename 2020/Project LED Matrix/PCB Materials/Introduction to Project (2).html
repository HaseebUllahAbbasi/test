Introduction to Project
This task of fabricating Led Matrix was carried out in FABLAB Sukkur IBA University. Our inspiration for making such interesting project was from Side Screen in Mercedes Benz stadium Atlanta, Georgia United States. Our final product is not of that size but is basic prototype of that masterpiece. This 24x24 led matrix will display Amazing Sprites and clock on it. All these need untiring efforts throughout Semester. The prototype we are thinking and trying to design as our project will look like.
