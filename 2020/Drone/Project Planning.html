Project Planning:

A proper and clever plan is essential for completion of any project with perfection. From the very first day of this project, it was in our mind that without teamwork completion of this project is not possible. We all, as a team, took our task seriously and distributed our project into parts so that it can be easy to work in different dimensions and to share our understanding with our team members, which is no doubt the best way to save time and get maximum yield. Some of our team members who were interested and were enthusiastic to work on code, took their part; some decided to work on circuit and so on.
